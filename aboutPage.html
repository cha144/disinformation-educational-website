Now for some beginner tips: (Hover over)

Too good? Maybe not true.

Keep an open mind!
If some sort of information seems way too out of the blue, chances are its fake. Seek out suspicious statements through each detail, whether it relates to numbers, people, places, and more.

No author? No credibility!

Look for any author/editor!
Look through the site and see if there are any authors or methods of communicating with those who wrote the article. Investigate further if needed.

Lateral Reading!

Don't stick to just one page!
Go on another tab and check out related articles to see if the information stays consistent. If there aren't any other major articles, chances are that the information is disinformation or fake-news.
